ABOUT US

Our Company

We are a leading local honey company founded in April 2020 and we pride ourselves on our high quality, minimally processed honey. With no added preservatives and harmful chemicals often found in other bee by products, our honey is completely uncontaminated and useful for your health. Our pure honey is harvested by skilled local beekeepers, many of whom have been impacted by this pandemic and we are immensely proud of the fact that we can provide enough economical support for them and their families.

Our Products

We offer three types of honey products for sale. Our first product is Madu Asli 3 in 1 , which is a special blend of pure honey, pollen bee and royal jelly. This mixture is rich in nutrients, vitamins and protein. Our second product is Madu Aneka Flora , this kind of honey is produced by the bees that get its nectarines from various range of flowers available in Indonesian forest. Our last product is Madu Kapuk Randu which is derived from Kapuk Randu flower. All of our products mentioned above are exceptionally nutritious and have proven to be beneficial for your health.

Our Certification

Golden Bees Honey went through rigorous lab tests to prove the pureness and realness of our honey. Back in August 2020 we passed each and every lab test with flying colors. Now, we can proudly say that our honey products passed the SUCOFINDO lab test as well as the SNI quality and authenticity test for honey. Therefore, we can assure you that our product is one of the highest quality honey accessible on the market.

OUR-PRODUCTS

These are our finest honey products, they are all free of added chemicals, minimally processed and packaged in a way that retains all the goodness of nature in one jar. Each product has its own uniqueness and you can combine them for full potency.

Madu 3 in 1

Madu Aneka Flora
Rp 60,000

Produced using the nectarines found from numerous types of flowers obtained by the bees in the Indonesian forest. This variation allows the honey to have a distinct and unique taste compared to any other type of honey. Madu Aneka Flora is also known for its anti-aging properties.

Produced exclusively using the nectarines of Kapuk Randu flowers. Amongst its known benefits for the body, Madu Kapuk Randu is especially good for the digestive system. This honey can also act as an excellent dietary supplement.
